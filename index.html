New Histogram-Based User and Item Profiles for Recommendation Systems

I am honored to be one of the authors of the research paper titled *"New Histogram-Based User and Item Profiles for Recommendation Systems,"* which we proudly published in IEEE in 2023. This paper presents a groundbreaking approach to recommendation systems by introducing innovative histogram-based techniques for modeling user and item profiles. Our methodology leverages statistical distributions and profile granularity to enhance the accuracy of recommendations while addressing scalability challenges in large-scale systems. The work represents a significant step forward in improving the personalization and efficiency of modern recommendation engines, and we are excited about the impact it can have on both academic research and real-world applications.
